About us

Who We Are

Edmik Tech Pvt. Ltd. is at the forefront of technological innovation with our latest project, Orange Network, which aims to revolutionize educational content access and delivery. We're not just creating a platform but spearheading a movement towards a more informed and connected world.

Our Mission

As a recognized and well-funded edtech organization, we focus on providing end-to-end tech solutions to educational institutes, especially in rural and semi-rural areas, making technology affordable and accessible. With recent international investment, we're poised for rapid growth.

Culture and Opportunities

We pride ourselves on a transparent and approachable management team, offering opportunities to individuals from diverse backgrounds. Our company culture celebrates learning, with many of our team members starting as interns and growing into full-time roles. We believe in empowering our employees, including those looking to rejoin the workforce, particularly women, fostering an environment of trust and growth.
